About Me

Reece Kelly is a concept artist and illustrator based in Glasgow, Scotland. He received his BA (Hons) degree in 2021 from Abertay University in Dundee, majoring in Digital Computer Arts. Reece's passion lies in creating strong and engaging designs that help tell stories from both himself and those around him, using a primarily digital medium with an influence from his love of role-playing games and animation.

Reece is looking to further better his craft, work with other storytellers and gain more experience in the creative world. Maybe even nerd out with friends in the process.
